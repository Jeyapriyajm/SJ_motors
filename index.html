Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our services or policies? Explore the answers to common inquiries about SJ Motors' offerings and operations.

What are the operating hours of SJ Motors?

We are open Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. On Sundays, we operate from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

Do you provide vehicle pickup and drop-off services?

Yes, SJ Motors offers complimentary vehicle pickup and drop-off within a 10-kilometer radius of our workshop. Additional charges may apply for longer distances.

What services are included in the standard maintenance package?

Our standard maintenance package includes oil changes, brake inspections, tire rotations, and a full vehicle health check. Additional services can be added upon request.
